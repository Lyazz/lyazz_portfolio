EcomSuite

EcomSuite is a premium commerce suite that unifies the storefront, admin, loyalty, and delivery tracking into one refined experience. It is designed to make shopping feel effortless, operations feel calm, and repeat buying feel natural from discovery to delivery.

  • Role: Product Engineer
  • Platform: Web storefront, admin, delivery tracking
  • Timeline: Ongoing

Overview

EcomSuite is an Algeria-ready ecommerce system designed for local delivery, COD operations, and multilingual shoppers. It combines the public storefront, an operational admin, loyalty automation, and delivery tracking in one unified workflow, so teams can launch quickly and manage orders with clarity.

Key Features

  • Multilingual storefront in FR/EN/AR with full RTL support.
  • Catalog management for variants, attributes, discounts, and quantity pricing.
  • COD-first checkout with coupons, loyalty points, and referral flows.
  • Shipping rules by wilaya and commune with carrier setup and delivery tracking.
  • Admin dashboards for order status, customer profiles, and performance insights.
  • Optional WhatsApp or SMS updates for order and delivery notifications.

Workflow

  • Customers browse with search and filters, then check out with COD and saved addresses.
  • Admins confirm orders, assign shipping, and monitor delivery status end to end.
  • Loyalty events and repeat purchases are tracked inside the same admin surface.

Tech Stack

  • Next.js powering the web experience.
  • Internationalized UI with RTL support for Arabic.
  • Operations tooling tailored to COD and local delivery workflows.

Integrations & Operations

  • Maystro delivery integration for automated shipment creation and status updates.
  • Wilaya-based pricing and logistics aligned to Algerian delivery workflows.
  • Optional local card payments (CIB/Edahabia) based on provider availability.

Highlights

  • 58 wilayas covered with configurable delivery pricing.
  • COD as the default payment flow for local commerce teams.
  • FR/EN/AR language coverage for regional shoppers.
